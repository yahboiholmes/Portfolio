Career Path

I began my professional journey in 2019 as a Mechanic Assistant at Automatetricks in Makati City, where I supported senior mechanics in diagnosing and repairing vehicle issues, conducted routine maintenance, and managed service records. In 2023, I transitioned into a customer-facing role as a Barista and Dining Staff at The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf in Taguig City, where I provided quality service, ensured cleanliness, and maintained a safe and organized workspace. In early 2024, I expanded into tech as a Backend Developer at Taguig Convention Center, applying skills in problem-solving, critical thinking, and time management while managing SQL queries and backend data handling. Here, I manage customer service, ticketing, loyalty programs, revenue accounting, disruption support, and data analytics, all within a fast-paced, global airline operations environment. Each of these roles has shaped my adaptability, strengthened my technical and interpersonal skills, and built a foundation for continued growth across diverse fields.
